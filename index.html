About The Website

This program is a part of our examination work in high school. The main focus of this website is to visualize and help students to picture what time dilation looks like. This can also be used by teachers all over the world in order to make it easier for their students to learn about interstellar travels and time dilation. As of today, scientists and astronauts that work in the scientific and space field are trying to explore space to expand our understanding of the universe and discover perhaps new life or a new civilization. However, us humans have a limited life span and limited resources for interstellar travel. In order to travel fast in a reasonable time frame we have to travel at the speed of light which currently is impossible. There are obstacles we have yet to overcome, obstacles like relativity and time dilatation. With this project we believe we can get a better understanding of these obstacles that keep us from achieving us to the stars and beyond. The created website whose purpose is to explain time dilation and the theory of relativity is meant to give better acknowledgement to the overall public. The website’s goal is to aid and provide both students and others the information they need to both calculate and understand the theories. One of the questions that this paper will answer is What time dilation is and How the program of the website is created.

Information text

Time Dilation Equation:

Δt' = γ * Δt0 = Δt0 / √(1 - v²/c²)

t0 is the time that has passed for a observer in movement.
(Time dilation can also be counted with the time of an observer
that is stationary, but has not been implemented on the website).

v is the speed for the travelling observer (m/s)

c is the speed o flight (299,792,458 m/s)
